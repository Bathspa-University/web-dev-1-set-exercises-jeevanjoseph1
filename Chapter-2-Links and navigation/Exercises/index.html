Assassin's Creed

Plot

"Assassin's Creed" is an action-adventure video game series developed and published by Ubisoft. The series features historical fiction mixed with real-world events and follows the age-old conflict between the Assassins, who fight for peace with free will, and the Templars, who desire peace through control. The plot often revolves around the use of a device called the Animus, which allows individuals to access the genetic memories of their ancestors.

The main storyline spans multiple games and historical periods, including the Third Crusade, the Italian Renaissance, the American Revolution, the Golden Age of Piracy, and more.
